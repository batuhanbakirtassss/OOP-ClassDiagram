-The classes "Horse", "Feline" (Felines) and "Rodent" (Rodents) represent species groupings. Each one is derived from the "Animal" class.

-Adds the "Zebra" and "Donkey" types into the "Horse" class. The "neigh()" method represents the specific behavior of horses.

-Adds the "Lion" and "Tiger" types into the "Feline" class. The "roar()" method represents the peculiar behavior of felines.

-Adds the "Mouse" (Rat) and "Beaver" (Beaver) types into the "Rodent" class. The "gnaw()" method represents the specific behavior of rodents.
